# Remove kill_port from production startup

## Problem
`main.rs` calls `lsof + kill -9` on startup to free ports. Runs unconditionally in production — can kill unrelated processes sharing a port by coincidence.

## What to build
- Remove `kill_port` entirely, or gate behind `AMELISO_DEV=true` env var
- In production, a port conflict should be a fatal startup error, not silently killed

## Files
- `server/src/main.rs` — `kill_port` fn and its two call sites
